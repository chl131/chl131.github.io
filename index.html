About This Place

This is a place where I can write and record something that is related to my hobbies. In the past, I sometimes wrote posts about music and audio on online communities or forums, and used Instagram to share the foods I ate with some short comments. However, the biggest problem I encountered is that it is extremely difficult to make the article looks like the way I want when writing on either social media or online fourms. Then I realized that the most straight-foward solution is:

Build one by myself so it can meet all my requirements.

Now there will be no more unreasonable rules and format constraints. Moreover, I can finally organize my articles with the logic I prefer. Oh, since this website is in its initial stage, some parts and features are still in progress. I'll make them online once they're finished.
